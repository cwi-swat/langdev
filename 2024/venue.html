Venue & Travel Information

Seville

Seville is a monumental city located at the South of Spain. Its well-known as the America's Gate during the XVI century as all the commerce between America and Spain was centralized in the city. Don't forget to reserve some time to visit the city and get lost on its ancient streets.

Tourism & landmarks to visit

  • Plaza de España
  • Real Alcazar de Sevilla
  • Seville Cathedral & La Giralda
  • Seville City Hall
  • Archivo General de Indias
  • Las Setas de Sevilla
  • Roman city of Itálica, Santiponce
  • More ideas
